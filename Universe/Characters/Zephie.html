Description

Zephie Yuumo Venturesome is a fiery kid who find it difficult to relate to people. She could never keep friends for very long, since kaiya children are taught to be afraid of half kaiya half Vaine children.

Pre-birth

Zephie's mother, Sarah Green Anderson, lived on Earth before being kidnapped by her boyfriend at the time, Jack. She was taken to Aoria to be used for genetic experiments by a scientist named Ace in an attempt to create a superhuman weapon. Jack intended to keep Sarah for himself after kidnapping her, but Ace refused to give heed to his demands. Jack sabatoged the facility, leading to Sarah's escape, eventually making her way to a small farm south of the blue mists.
