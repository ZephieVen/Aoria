Description

Ace is a scientist currenty living and working in (insert city name). His healing skills are exceptional and strangely will only accept favors as payment. He is the creator of Zephie by means of genetic alterations of a fetus carried by Sarah Green Anderson.

Past

He grew up in (insert place name), a planet in a another dimension, ruled by a tyranical superhuman named Kyron. When he was very young, Kyron altered his genetic code, giving him hyper inteligence. He soon realized that the world he lived in caused much pain and suffering and ran away to find a group of rebels when he was 10. The rebels were skeptical but eventually allowed him to help them due to his usefulness. He lived with them until he was ~17. After that he was kidnapped back by Kyron and was given cybernetic implant to transform him into a soldier to attack the rebels who raised him. Kyron did this for entertainment and the rebels realized that all of their progress was fabricated by Kyron. Ace resisted the urge to kill the rebels, telling them to leave, and instead self-combusted leaving a large building destroyed. The rebels believed that he was killed, but he was temporally and spacially displaced on Earth in May 13th 1873.
