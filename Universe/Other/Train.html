Description

_Trains_ are very important in the world of Aoria. They connect the countires of Rystallia and Ceoten allowing for ease of transport and trade. Some types of _trains_ are meant to carry passengers, others cargo, and others have various other purposes.

Appearance

Passenger

Often in silver or whites, these _trains_ are regularaly cleaned and maintained, symbolizing the good relations of the two countries it connects.

History

Non-locomotive

_Trains_ used to be simple wheeled objects on rails, using gravity and varous animals to aid in their movement. These _trains_ were ideal for life in Ceoten, due to the various plateaus, hills, and dunes that allowed the builders to start at a high point and travel longer distances. There have been various ancient attempts to make _trains_ in Rystallia, but due to snow and ice they were always untenable.
