Description

Halflings are when a Kaiyane and another species have a child. At least two genetic donors must have special abilities. Halflings often have memory problems. Halflings go through a period when they turn 16-20 years old where they get weak and all of their previous unresolved injuries and traumas are worsened. The first Halfling to live past 20 was Typhe in 1818.

Appearance

Halflings have a star shape somewhere on their face. They are more commonly short.
